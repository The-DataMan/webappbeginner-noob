Rules of the guessing game.

This is a geography guessing game, you will have to guess the name of a country within 30 seconds, based a few things, first you will go like a hangman game you will be give the length of the nameof the country, and a letter in a random position, after that you will get a few hints, at the end of the game you will be shown the flag and then a random information about the country.
